Brownies

Descriptions

A brownie is a square or rectangular chocolate baked confection. Brownies come in a variety of forms and may be either fudgy or cakey, depending on their density. They may include nuts, frosting, cream cheese, chocolate chips, or other ingredients.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/3 cup unsweetened cocoa powder

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Grease and flour an 8-inch square pan.
  3. In a large saucepan, melt 1/2 cup butter.
  4. Remove from heat, and stir in sugar, eggs, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
  5. Beat in 1/3 cup cocoa, 1/2 cup flour, salt, and baking powder.
